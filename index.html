🍹 Our story

About ATX Yummy Face

Built by Austinites, for Austinites.

ATX Yummy Face started as a simple Google Maps list — one person's obsessive notes on every happy hour deal worth knowing in Austin. It grew fast. Friends wanted it. Strangers asked to share it. So we built this.

Why we exist

Austin has hundreds of incredible bars and restaurants running genuinely great happy hour deals every single day. But finding them is chaos — buried in Instagram stories, outdated Yelp pages, or just word of mouth. We think that's fixable.

ATX Yummy Face is a single, clean, searchable guide to the best deals in the city. We keep it updated, we keep it honest, and we keep it free for diners forever.

How it works

Venues pay $5/month to be listed and keep their deals up to date. Diners search for free. We verify submissions and review flagged listings to keep the data accurate. No ads. No affiliate links. No sponsored rankings.

Who we are

A small Austin-based team that goes to a lot of happy hours in the name of research. We're always adding venues, fixing errors, and improving the experience. Got feedback? We'd love to hear from you.

❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about ATX Yummy Face.

Is ATX Yummy Face free to use? ›
Yes — completely free for diners, always. You can search, filter, and browse all listings without creating an account or paying anything.
How do I get my bar listed? ›
Click "List your bar" in the navigation and fill out the form. Listings cost $5/month — no setup fees, no contracts. We'll review your submission and have you live within 24 hours.
How do I update my listing? ›
Click "Update a listing" in the navigation. Fill in your venue name, your role, and the updated deal information. We'll review and publish changes within 24 hours.
How accurate are the deals? ›
We do our best to keep everything current, but happy hour deals can change without notice. We always recommend confirming deals with the venue before visiting. If you spot something outdated, please report it using the "Report outdated info" link.
Can I cancel my listing? ›
Yes, anytime. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. Just email us at venues@atxhappyhour.com and we'll remove your listing within 24 hours.
How do I report a closed or incorrect venue? ›
Use the "Report outdated info" link in the footer or Help section. We review all reports and update the listing within 24 hours. Your name and email are optional — you can report anonymously.
Do you have a mobile app? ›
Not yet! The website is mobile-friendly so it works great in your phone's browser. A native app is on our roadmap.
How do I advertise on ATX Yummy Face? ›
We offer featured listings, newsletter sponsorships, and category sponsorships. Visit the Advertise page in the footer to see options and get in touch.
I'm a journalist — who do I contact? ›
Reach out to press@atxhappyhour.com. We respond to all press inquiries within 24 hours.

Privacy policy

Last updated: May 2026

ATX Yummy Face ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ATX Yummy Face website. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information we collect

From diners: We do not require account creation. If you submit a report or sign up for our newsletter, we collect your name and email address voluntarily provided.

From venue owners: When submitting or updating a listing, we collect your name, email, phone number, role, and venue details. This information is used solely to manage your listing.

Automatically: We may collect standard web analytics data (page views, browser type, general location) to understand how the site is used. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to operate and improve the service, communicate with you about your listing or submission, send our weekly newsletter (if you've subscribed), and respond to inquiries. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to our weekly email, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. We will not share your email address with third parties.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting core site functionality.

Data retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the service. Venue owners may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us at hello@atxhappyhour.com.

Third-party services

Our map feature uses OpenStreetMap and CartoDB, which are subject to their own privacy policies. We do not share personal data with these providers.

Children's privacy

ATX Yummy Face is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll note the date of the last update at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
